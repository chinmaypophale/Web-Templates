Payment terms

We operate with a “net 60” payment arrangement for approved purchase orders. That is, payment is expected in full no more than 60 days after your order is processed. Currently, we cover shipping costs for educators.

Note:

  • Please allow 1-2 business days for us to set up your account (if applicable) and process your order.
  • It will take up to 7 business days to deliver via FedEx ground. As such, we highly recommend ordering by email, as it is the fastest way to process your order. If you need your order sooner, please email [email protected] for other shipping options, including FedEx 2nd day and overnight.
  • Please do not send in duplicate orders. Email us at [email protected]
