  • Cafe MH12- Birth- The Beginning

    September 2012, the journey began from Rasta Peth- one of authentic foodie places of Pune. We used to serve a lots of dishes ranging from quick bites, munchies, milkshakes, main course and desserts, hence the name Café MH12. Sole Motto was to serve healthy food quickly & within budget. Over a period of time customers started praising our Pav Bhaji, Pizza, Pulao, and Milkshakes, so we kept only our bestseller dishes and Pav Bhaji being the signature dish, we renamed the Restaurant to MH12 Pav Bhaji.

  • MH12 Pav Bhaji- Being Mature

    With the brand makeover came lots of challenges. Every day we’ve improved ourselves to serve you better, as it is said “It takes 20 years to build a reputation and five minutes to ruin it". We kept on doing R&D on our dishes to reduce the preparation time without reducing the quality of the food item. Hence we are in a position to operate as a Quick Service Restaurant (QSR).

    Super Car Based Service Speed- Pav Bhaji & Pulao was served under a minute to the customer.

    With Good Hygiene Comes Great Quality- Got Certified from Equinox Labs (FSSAI Approved Lab) for Food Hygiene Certification.

    Pav Bhaji in Kilos- We were & are the only ones who serve Pav Bhaji in Kilos and that too at an affordable price, which is even cheaper than Vada Pav. This was a revolutionary step to propel us to great heights.

  • MH12 Pav Bhaji - Being King

    We started expanding our presence in Pune. Opened our first outlet in Moshi in 2017 and we haven’t looked back. We want to give our customers the true experience of our Pav Bhaji & other dishes which is healthy, hygienic and cooked in Amul Butter. We now have 11 Outlets throughout Maharashtra & Gujrat and plans are set for Pan India presence by 2025.
